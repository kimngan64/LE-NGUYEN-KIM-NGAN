    UNIFY is all about the concept of chemistry. Established since 2019, combining influences curated from science and subversive culture, we express the fashion philosophy and experimental spirit with innovative garment constructions. We are “Chemists”. Chemists mix different substances to create new matters, that’s exactly what we do. We focus on trailblazing fabric materials and processes, testing and tailoring cultural theories, analyzing compound of apparels from physical and visual properties to the final uniquely crafted synthesis forming a peculiar style. Each of our collection is a reactant processed from a statement to the world with a distinct aesthetic perspective under the universe context. We are visionaries and scientists that create future.
